About Medina of Sousse

The Medina of Sousse is one of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Tunisia. It is an outstanding example of a fortified Muslim city, demonstrating the layout of a typical medina. The Medina has preserved its original structure and charm, featuring narrow winding streets, traditional souks, and significant historical monuments such as the Ribat, the Grand Mosque, and the Kasbah. Walking through the Medina is like stepping back in time, offering a glimpse into the rich cultural and architectural heritage of the region.
